About

      At the age of 18, I joined the U.S. Marine Corps and was soon deployed to Iraq. I got out with an honorable discharge in 2010. At that point I began to attend NewSchool of Architecture & Design in downtown San Diego. After careful consideration, I realized Architecture was not my true calling and decided to leave. With a strong passion for designing things could captivate the user, I decided to learn programming and pursue design as a web developer.
